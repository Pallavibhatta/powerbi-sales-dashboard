## Business Insights

- **[Order Timeliness](ca://s?q=Analyze_order_timeliness_trends)**: Monthly analysis shows a clear distinction between delayed and on-time orders, highlighting operational inefficiencies that need attention.  
- **[Regional Sales Performance](ca://s?q=Evaluate_regional_sales_performance)**: State-wise sales indicate stronger performance in SP compared to RJ and MG, suggesting targeted strategies could boost weaker regions.  
- **[Seasonal Trends](ca://s?q=Identify_seasonal_sales_trends)**: Revenue varies across seasons, with peaks in certain periods. This insight can guide inventory planning and promotional campaigns.  
- **[Yearly Growth](ca://s?q=Assess_yearly_revenue_growth)**: The upward revenue trend demonstrates overall business growth, validating current strategies while encouraging further scaling.  
- **[Category Analysis](ca://s?q=Analyze_sales_by_category)**: Delayed vs on-time orders by category reveal inefficiencies in specific sectors like agro industry and commerce, pointing to areas for process improvement.

## How to Use

- **[Download Report](ca://s?q=Download_ShopNest_Report_docx)**: Click the link in the *Project Report* section to access the detailed analysis in `ShopNest_Report.docx`.  
- **[Open Dashboard](ca://s?q=Open_Power_BI_dashboard_file)**: Clone or download this repository to your local machine.  
- **[Install Power BI](ca://s?q=Install_Power_BI_Desktop)**: Ensure you have Power BI Desktop installed to view and interact with the dashboard.  
- **[Load Data](ca://s?q=Load_data_into_Power_BI)**: Open the `.pbix` file (if included) or connect the dashboard to the provided dataset.  
- **[Explore Visuals](ca://s?q=Explore_Power_BI_visuals)**: Use filters and slicers to drill down into specific regions, categories, or time periods.  
- **[Refresh Data](ca://s?q=Refresh_data_in_Power_BI)**: If connected to live sources, refresh the dashboard to see the latest updates.
